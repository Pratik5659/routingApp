About Our Angular Application

Our Angular application is designed to manage products efficiently, providing users with features to add new products, view them in a card format, and explore detailed product information seamlessly.

Features Covered in the Application

  • Product Management: Users can add products directly from the home page and view them in a structured card layout on the Product List page.
  • Routing Implementation: The application uses Angular routing extensively to navigate between different sections, including child routing for detailed product views.

Detailed Explanation of Routing Implementation

Routing in our Angular application facilitates smooth navigation between various views, ensuring users can easily switch between the home page, product list, and detailed product information pages without losing context.

Conclusion

Our Angular application exemplifies effective use of Angular's routing capabilities, enhancing user experience and showcasing the framework's versatility in managing complex application structures.
